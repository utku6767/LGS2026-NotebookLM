## 1. Soru Tipi: Paragraf / Özellik Eşleştirme (Personal Traits)
**Soru Kökü:** Read the text and answer the question.

Hi, I am Sarah. I have a best friend, Emily. We share all our secrets because I know she never tells them to anyone. She always backs me up when I need her. Also, she easily makes friends because she always smiles and makes jokes. However, she never changes her mind.

Which of the following does NOT describe Emily?
**Şıklar:**
- A) Reliable
- B) Amusing
- C) Stubborn
- D) Laid-back
**Doğru Cevap ve Çözüm:** **Cevap: D.** Metinde Emily'nin sır tuttuğu ve destek olduğu (reliable), insanları güldürdüğü (amusing) ve fikrini hiç değiştirmediği (stubborn) belirtilmiştir. Rahat/umursamaz (laid-back) olduğuna dair bir bilgi yoktur.

## 2. Soru Tipi: Davet ve Mazeret (Accepting, Refusing, Excuses)
**Soru Kökü:** Mark: I am organizing a movie night at my house on Sunday. Would you like to join?

Tom: That sounds awesome, but my cousins are coming over.
Lisa: I'd love to, but I can't.
John: Great idea! I will bring some popcorn.
Anna: I am sorry, but I have to finish my science project.

Who refuses the invitation with NO excuse?
**Şıklar:**
- A) Tom
- B) Lisa
- C) John
- D) Anna
**Doğru Cevap ve Çözüm:** **Cevap: B.** Tom kuzenlerinin geleceğini, Anna projesi olduğunu belirterek mazeret sunmuştur. John daveti kabul etmiştir. Lisa reddetmiş fakat herhangi bir mazeret belirtmemiştir.

## 3. Soru Tipi: Yemek Tarifi ve Pişirme Yöntemleri (In The Kitchen)
**Soru Kökü:** Read the recipe and answer the question.

How to make an omelette:
First, put some oil in a pan and heat it.
Second, crack two eggs into a bowl and mix them.
Then, add some salt and cheese into the bowl.
After that, pour the mixture into the hot pan.
Finally, cook it for 3 minutes and serve hot.

Which of the following questions does NOT have an answer in the recipe?
**Şıklar:**
- A) How long does it take to cook?
- B) What are the ingredients?
- C) Which cooking method do we use?
- D) How much cheese do we need?
**Doğru Cevap ve Çözüm:** **Cevap: D.** Metinde pişirme süresi (3 minutes), malzemeler (oil, eggs, salt, cheese) ve pişirme yöntemi (hot pan / fry) yer almaktadır. Ancak ne kadar peynir ekleneceği belirtilmemiştir.
